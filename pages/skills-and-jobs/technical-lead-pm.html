Technical Lead/Project Manager

JOB TITLE: Tech Lead
JOB TYPE: Full-Time
LOCATION: Remote Work
SUPERVISOR/MANAGER: Hex Clan Manager

    MAIN DUTIES/RESPONSIBILITIES:
  • Establishment, implementation and in detail guidance of recruitment and training programs.
  • Throughout challenges and complications, constantly edifying and encouraging team members while simultaneously contributing any knowledge.
  • Delicately assigning tasks according to the skillset and competency of every team member.
  • Detailed management of deadlines and task progress, whilst simultaneously reassigning according to unexpected complications or unforeseen levels of difficulty that may arise.
  • Searching for third party software's that may assist team members in any way possible.
  • Supervising team meetings and assigning goals/aims for each individual team member as well as the group as a whole.
  • Moderate collaboration with team members according to the technical knowledge necessary to complete certain tasks.
  • Code health review and management according to coding standards and guidelines.
  • Detailed translation of business requirements to individuals within the programming team.
